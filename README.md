# 🚖 Uber Booking Analytics Dashboard

## 📖 Project Overview

The **Uber Booking Analytics Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze booking trends, revenue performance, ride completion rates, customer behavior, vehicle utilization, and payment preferences. The dashboard helps stakeholders gain valuable insights into ride operations and make data-driven business decisions.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze Uber booking performance and ride trends.
- Monitor booking status and ride completion rates.
- Understand customer payment preferences.
- Evaluate vehicle type performance.
- Identify operational inefficiencies and growth opportunities.
- Visualize ride demand patterns over time.

---

## 📈 Key Insights

- Total bookings exceeded **149K rides**.
- Completed rides account for the majority of bookings.
- UPI and Cash are the most preferred payment methods.
- Go Mini and Premier Sedan are among the most frequently booked vehicle types.
- Booking demand remains relatively consistent throughout the year.
- A small percentage of rides are cancelled by drivers, customers, or due to unavailable drivers.
